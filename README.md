# NeuroSales — Complete AI-Powered Sales & Distribution Automation Suite

## Product Overview

NeuroSales is an advanced, AI-driven Sales & Distribution Automation platform designed to transform how companies manage field operations, retail execution, merchandising, and last-mile delivery.

The platform connects every layer of the sales ecosystem into one intelligent digital infrastructure:

- Sales Representatives (SR)
- Delivery Teams
- Retailers
- Merchandisers
- Supervisors
- Management Teams

By combining mobile applications, web-based management systems, AI automation, and real-time analytics, NeuroSales enables organizations to execute sales operations with greater accuracy, visibility, compliance, and operational speed.
